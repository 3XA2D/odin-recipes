Roasted Zucchini Casserole

Description

Roasted zucchini with onion and layered with marinara sauce and pasta. A meal-in-one with a salad and rolls. Can't get better than that! A great way to use up your zucchini crop in the summer.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium zucchinis, sliced
  • 2 medium onions, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar marinara sauce
  • Â½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 400 deg Fahrenheit
  2. toss zucchinis and onions in a large bowl with olive oil until evenly coated
  3. spread vegetables into a large baking dish
  4. roast vegetables in the preheated oven until golden or for about 30 minutes
  5. season with salt and pepper
  6. reduce oven heat to 350 deg Fahrenheit
  7. fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil
  8. stir in penne and return to a boil
  9. cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but still firm to the bite or for about 11 minutes
  10. when the pasta is done drain it
  11. lightly grease a 9" x 13" baking dish
  12. heat marinara sauce in a saucepan over medium heat until sauce is hot
  13. spread hot marinara sauce into the prepared baking dish
  14. followed by penne pasta and zucchini mixture. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese atop vegetables
  15. bake in the preheated oven until sauce is bubbly or for about 35 to 40 minutes
  16. allow casserole to rest for 5 minutes before serving
