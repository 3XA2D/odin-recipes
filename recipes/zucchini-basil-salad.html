Zucchini Basil Salad

Description

This is the perfect recipe if you have a lot of zucchini to use up. It is important to thinly slice the zucchini, so I use the slicer on my box grater. I sometimes fill 4 baking sheets at a time. You can eat it warm or cold. I will eat this portion all by myself.

Ingredients

  • 2 thinly sliced zucchini
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons wort of finely chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 400 deg Fahrenheit
  2. grease 2 baking sheet with olive oil
  3. combine thinly sliced zucchini and olive oil in a bowl
  4. spread the sliced zucchini in a single layer on the baking sheets
  5. roast in the preheated onen until the zucchini are soft and lightly brown on the edges or for about 20 to 25 minutes
  6. transfer to a bowl and allow to cool slightly
  7. season with salt and pepper
  8. add the basil and mix
  9. serve warm or cold
